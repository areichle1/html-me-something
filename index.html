I went to Puerto Rico with my family in 2008

Castillo San Felipe del Morro

We went to visit this citadel in San Juan. It was built in the 16th century. We explored and found a lot of hidden spots within it.

El Yunque Rainforest

We drove up into the El Yunque rainforest. The waterfalls were beautiful and it was raining the whole time we were up on the mountain.

Culebra

We took a day trip and went to the island of Culebra. It is located 17 miles off the coast of Puerto Rico. We went to a beach there and there were hardly any people.

Surfing Lessons

We took surfing lessons. I had never surfed before trying it on this trip and I really enjoyed it.
